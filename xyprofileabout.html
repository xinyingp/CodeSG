Using basic HTML and CSS skills, this site was built from scratch to serve as a platform for me to share my thoughts and interest in digital marketing and analytics.

I started to discover my interest in digital analytics ever since I was exposed to the magic of Google Analytics. I love looking into user journey to filter and discover unobservable and deeper insights to drive brand's performance and success.

From there, I decided to take my interest further by taking on Analytics modules in school, where I enjoyed learning how to visualise data using Tableau; found value in conducting clustering, market basket analysis and text mining using SAS; and created differentiated strategies for various customer profiles based on regression analysis using SPSS.

When it is time to take a break, you can find me working out, or going on a food expedition with my friends.
